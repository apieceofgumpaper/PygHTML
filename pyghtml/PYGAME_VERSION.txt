PygHTML Runtime Pins
===================

Runtime bundle: pygame-web/archives (folder 0.9) vendored under:
  engine/vendor/archives/0.9

Interpreter: CPython 3.12 (WASM build: cpython312)

Pygame build: pygame-ce compiled to WebAssembly and shipped in that bundle.
The distribution name used by the upstream project is `pygame_static` (wheel version 1.0).
